• About

PDX InTransit is mobile app to track arrivals for Portland, OR's Trimet public transit system, and find stops/routes nearby. Originally started as a personal solution to finding stops quickly, PDX InTransit soon became a fully-fledged transit tracker. Never be lost again!

Designed, developed, and published by Will Acton. All rights reserved.
